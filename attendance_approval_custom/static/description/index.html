Employee Attendance Approval Workflow .

Key Features:

  • Allow the manager to approve attendance as shown.
  • Allow the manager to reject attendance with reason as shown.
  • Allow them to approve and reject attendance from a list view for multiple attendance records together.
  • Allow them to approve and reject multiple attendances from the mass approve attendance and mass reject attendance button.
  • For more details please check below screenshots and watch the video.

Overview

  • Approve/Reject Button on Attendance Form .
  • After click on approve button approved by field fill up automatically.
  • Approval Details added on Attendance Form.
  • Approve Attendance and Reject Attendance added in action .
  • Open Mass Reject Attendance Wizard.
  • On click of Approve Attendance Button open Wizard to approve Attendance.
  • Set as Draft, Approve , Reject buttons added on list view.
  • Rejected by and Rejected reason field fill up..
